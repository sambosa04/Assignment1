Meet the Teacher

Ms. Emma Williams

Ms. Emma has been teaching at Sunshine Kindergarten for 10 years. With a degree in Early Childhood Education and a passion for fostering a love of learning in young children, she brings joy and creativity to every class.

Her favorite activities include leading nature walks, organizing fun craft projects, and reading stories that spark the imagination. She believes every child has the potential to succeed and strives to create a warm, inclusive classroom where every child can thrive.
